Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EngageHub collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect account details (such as email and profile identifiers), workspace data, connected social account metadata, and content/analytics required to provide the Service.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to authenticate users, connect social accounts, publish content on your behalf, provide analytics, improve performance, and maintain security and compliance.

3. Third-Party Services

When you connect third-party platforms (e.g., TikTok), EngageHub processes tokens and APIs only for authorized features. Third-party providers process data under their own policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard safeguards to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continuously improve our controls.

5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the Service, meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6. Your Choices

You can disconnect social accounts and request account/data deletion subject to legal and operational obligations.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: privacy@engagehub.co.za
